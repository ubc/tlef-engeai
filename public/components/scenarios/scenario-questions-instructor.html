Not sure how to format answer cards?

How answer cards work

Each flashcard is one swipeable step students see after “See the Answer” or exam submit. The answer key is plain markdown; we split it into cards automatically.

Recommended structure

  • # Card 1: Title starts a new card (official delimiter).
  • Everything under that heading until the next # Card is the card body.
  • ## Topic and ### Highlight are optional sections inside a card — they render in the body, they do not create extra cards.
  • Legacy fallbacks still work: ## Step headings, or a numbered list (1. 2. 3.).

Why this layout

  • One clear card boundary (#) so instructors always know what students will swipe next.
  • ## / ### stay free for structure inside a single card (topic + highlight).
  • Avoids treating every heading level as a separate card.
