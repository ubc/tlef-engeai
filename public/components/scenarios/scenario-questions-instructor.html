How answer cards work

Each flashcard is one swipeable step students see after “See the Answer” or exam submit. The answer key is plain markdown with LaTeX; we split it into cards automatically. The live card preview sits under the answer editor as you type.

Recommended structure

  • # Title starts a new card.
  • Everything under that heading until the next # is the card body (markdown + LaTeX like $Q = mc_p\\Delta T$).
  • Do not use ## / ### for cards — only # starts a new step.
  • The answer key must start with # Title. Leading prose or a missing first title leaves the carousel empty.

Why this layout

  • One clear card boundary (#) so instructors always know what students will swipe next.
  • Body stays free for equations and short prose — no extra Topic/Highlight scaffolding.
