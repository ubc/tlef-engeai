Learning Objectives

What are Learning Objectives?

Learning objectives are clear, measurable statements that describe what students will be able to do after completing a lesson or session. The learning objective is used to guide AI to create better socratic flow in the conversation with the AI.

Learning Objective Structure:

Title: A brief, descriptive title for the objective
Description: A detailed explanation of what students will learn

Learning Objectives Guide

Learning objectives should be:

  • Specific: Clearly state what students will learn
  • Measurable: Include observable outcomes
  • Achievable: Realistic for the time and resources available
  • Relevant: Aligned with course goals and student needs

Example: "Students will be able to calculate the heat transfer coefficient for a given system using Fourier's law."

Document Upload Guide

You can upload various types of materials:

  • PDFs: Lecture notes, readings, assignments
  • Presentations: PowerPoint slides, lecture materials
  • Documents: Word docs, text files, handouts
  • URLs: Links to videos, articles, online resources

All materials will be processed and made searchable for the AI tutor.
