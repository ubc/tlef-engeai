Learning Objectives

How to create and manage learning objectives for your sessions

What are Learning Objectives?

Learning objectives are clear, measurable statements that describe what students will be able to do after completing a session. They help guide both teaching and learning.

Example Learning Objectives:

  • Understand: "Students will understand the principles of thermodynamics"
  • Apply: "Students will be able to apply the first law of thermodynamics to solve problems"
  • Analyze: "Students will analyze the efficiency of different heat engines"

How to Add Learning Objectives

1

Navigate to a Session

Click on any division (week/topic) to expand it and see the sessions

2

Find the Learning Objectives Section

Each session has a "Learning Objectives" section with a count of current objectives

3

Click to Expand

Click on the "Learning Objectives" header to expand the section

4

Add New Objective

Fill in the "Objective Title" and "Objective Description" fields, then click "Add Objective"

üí° Tips for Writing Good Learning Objectives

  • Use action verbs (understand, apply, analyze, evaluate, create)
  • Be specific and measurable
  • Focus on what students will be able to do, not what you will teach
  • Keep them concise but clear
  • Align with your course learning outcomes
