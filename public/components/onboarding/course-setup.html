Welcome to EngE-AI!

This onboarding process will guide you through setting up your course. Each step is designed to be simple and straightforward.

What you'll need:

  • Your course information
  • Course organization preferences

Take your time with each step - you can always go back to make changes.

Enter your course name in the text field.

Type the full course name (e.g., CHBE 241 - Material and Energy Balances). The system will check if this course has already been initiated. If it has, you'll need to choose a different course name.

Tips:

  • Use the exact course name as it appears in your syllabus
  • Each course can only be initiated once in the system

Choose how to organize your course content.

By Week: Organize content chronologically by weeks (recommended for semester courses)

By Topic: Organize content by subject matter (recommended for modular courses)

This choice affects how content is presented to students and how you'll manage the course structure.

Specify the number of sections for your course.

For weekly organization: Enter the number of weeks (1-14)

For topic organization: Enter the number of topics (1-52)

This determines how many content sections you'll have to manage. You can always adjust this later.

Review all your selections before completing setup.

This final step shows a summary of all your choices. Please verify:

  • Course name is correct
  • Organization type and count are appropriate

Click "Complete Setup" to finish the onboarding process.

Course Setup Complete!

Congratulations! You have successfully completed the course setup process.

What you've accomplished:

  • Configured your course name and structure
  • Selected your content organization method
  • Determined the number of course sections

You're now ready to proceed to the next step: adding instructors and TAs, then setting up your course content and learning materials.
