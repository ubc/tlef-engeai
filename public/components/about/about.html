About EngE-AI

EngE-AI is an AI-powered study assistant platform designed specifically for chemical and material engineering students at UBC. Our platform provides guided critical thinking support following Facione's model, generates context-rich real-world troubleshooting scenarios, and acts as a 24/7 virtual tutor for CHBE and MTRL engineering courses.

Our Mission

We aim to enhance engineering education by providing students with an intelligent study companion that guides them through problem-solving rather than providing direct answers. Through the Socratic method, students develop critical thinking skills essential for engineering practice.

Key Features

  • Guided Learning: Uses the Socratic method to help students discover solutions
  • Engineering Focus: Specialized for chemical and material engineering concepts
  • Interactive Diagrams: Visual learning with process flows and engineering diagrams
  • Canvas Integration: Seamlessly embedded within UBC's learning management system
  • 24/7 Availability: Always-available study assistance
  • Context-Aware: Understands course materials and provides relevant guidance
